Welcome to SkyWhale SPACE!




What is Skywhale Space?
Recently, some of skywhales have taken an interest in studying celestial bodies!
So, we added a functionality so that you can input any longitude and latitude,
and the skywhales will tell you what the current moon phase is,
as well as the time of the next sunrise and sunset!
As the skywhales get better at studying celestial bodies, more funtionalities will be added. :)
