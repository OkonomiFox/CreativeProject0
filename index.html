This page is for nerds who play tabletop games like Dungeons and Dragons or Pathfinder!
Many of these games require the use of a variety of dice to play.
The most commonly used die for these games is called a "d20",
which is shorthand to mean a twenty-sided die.
This webpage includes all of the common dice needed for these types of games!
So go ahead, press some buttons and get some random numbers!
