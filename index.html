Why Should You Trust SkyWhale Weather Services?
If you're anything like the creator of this webpage, you never go outside.
What better way to know what it's like outside than to ask a skywhale who is outside all of the time?
Our flocks of semi-psychic skywhales can not only tell you what the weather is currently in your area,
they can also predict future weather with high levels of accuracy!
Skywhales love it when you ask the weather, so go ahead!
They will send psychic brain-wave data to our servers where it will br routed to you!
Just enter your city in the search bar above!
(Keep in mind that our skywhales usually like to fly above America because America is the land of freedom,
so weather forecasts for some other countries may not be available)
